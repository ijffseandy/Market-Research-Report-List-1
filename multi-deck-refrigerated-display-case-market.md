<p><p>The Multi-Deck Refrigerated Display Case market is categorized into two main types: Plug-In and Semi Plug-In display cases. Plug-In Multi-Deck Refrigerated Display Cases are standalone units that operate independently, typically requiring a standard electrical outlet, making them ideal for convenience stores and supermarkets. In contrast, Semi Plug-In Multi-Deck Refrigerated Display Cases are designed to operate more efficiently by connecting to a centralized refrigeration system, providing a balance of energy efficiency and performance, often used in larger retail environments.</p></p>
