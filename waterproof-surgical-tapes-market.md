<p><ul><li>PE</li><li>Non-Woven Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof surgical tapes market is primarily categorized into two types: PE (Polyethylene) and Non-Woven Fabric tapes. PE tapes are made from a plastic material that offers excellent water resistance, ensuring a secure barrier for wounds while allowing flexibility and conformability. Non-Woven Fabric tapes, on the other hand, are breathable and composed of polyester or polypropylene, providing gentle adhesion and comfort on the skin. Both types play crucial roles in medical applications, ensuring effective wound care and patient safety.</p></p>
