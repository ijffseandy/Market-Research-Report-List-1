<p><p>The nephrology drugs market encompasses various types that target kidney-related conditions. ACE inhibitors help manage hypertension and protect kidney function by relaxing blood vessels. Calcium channel blockers also reduce blood pressure but work by preventing calcium from entering heart and blood vessel cells. Beta blockers lower heart rate and reduce blood pressure, aiding in kidney protection. Diuretics promote fluid elimination, crucial for managing conditions like edema in kidney disease. Other medications may include medications for renal anemia or mineral bone disorders.</p></p>
