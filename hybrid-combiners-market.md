<p><strong>The Hybrid Combiners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>90-Degree Hybrid Combiners</li><li>180-Degree Hybrid Combiners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid combiners are essential components in RF and microwave systems, primarily used for signal processing and combining multiple signals. The 90-degree hybrid combiner, also known as a 4-port coupler, enables equal power division with a 90-degree phase shift, ideal for applications requiring balanced signal distribution and combining. In contrast, the 180-degree hybrid combiner, or Lange coupler, facilitates in-phase signal combining, often used in power amplifiers for increased efficiency and reduced distortion. Both types serve critical functions in communication systems.</p></p>
