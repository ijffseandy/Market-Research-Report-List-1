<p><strong>The Portable Space Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convector Heater</li><li>Radiant Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable space heater market primarily includes convection heaters and radiant heaters. Convector heaters work by heating air, creating a warm environment through convection currents. They are often used for whole-room heating and are relatively quiet. Radiant heaters, on the other hand, emit infrared radiation, directly warming objects and people in their vicinity. They provide instant heat and are ideal for spot heating. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, offering efficiency and flexibility in heating solutions.</p></p>
