<p><p>The industrial magnetic reed switch market can be categorized into three main types: Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A switches are normally open, closing the circuit when a magnetic field is applied. Form B switches are normally closed, opening the circuit when exposed to a magnetic field. Form C switches provide both functionalities, featuring a normally open and a normally closed contact, thus offering versatility in applications. These distinctions cater to various industrial automation and control requirements.</p></p>
