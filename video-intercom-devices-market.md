<p><p>The video intercom devices market is categorized primarily into two types: Analog and IP. In contrast, IP video intercoms utilize internet protocol technology, allowing for advanced features such as remote access, higher video resolution, and integration with smart home systems. The IP type is gaining popularity due to its scalability and flexibility, catering to modern security and communication needs.</p></p>
