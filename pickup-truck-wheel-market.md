Casting involves pouring molten metal into molds, creating complex shapes with good surface finishes, ideal for mass production. Forging, on the other hand, involves deforming metal under high pressure, resulting in stronger, lighter wheels. Other methods include processes like machining and extrusion, which may be used to produce specialized or customized wheels. Each type caters to different performance, weight, and cost requirements.</p></p>
