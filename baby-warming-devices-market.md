<p><ul><li>Phototherapy Units</li><li>Radiant Warmers</li><li>Incubators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baby warming devices market comprises three primary segments: phototherapy units, radiant warmers, and incubators. Phototherapy units are designed to treat neonatal jaundice using blue light to reduce bilirubin levels in infants. Radiant warmers provide a controlled thermal environment for premature or ill babies, offering warmth without enclosing the baby. Incubators create a stable setting for vulnerable infants, maintaining temperature and humidity while protecting them from external elements. Each type plays a critical role in neonatal care and improving infant health outcomes.</p></p>
