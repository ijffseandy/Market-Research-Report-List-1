<p><p>A Wireless Mesh Network (WMN) is a communication system made up of multiple interconnected nodes, allowing data to be transmitted across various devices within the network. The technology significantly reduces the need for extensive wiring and infrastructure, making it both cost-effective and scalable. </p><p>The Wireless Mesh Network Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for reliable connectivity solutions in urban environments and enterprises. As organizations seek to improve their network resilience and optimize coverage, the demand for mesh networking solutions continues to rise.
