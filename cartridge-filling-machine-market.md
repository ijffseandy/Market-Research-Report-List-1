<p><p>The cartridge filling machine market is categorized into three main types: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual machines. Fully-automatic machines offer high efficiency and speed, automating the entire filling process with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input but still enhance productivity compared to manual options, balancing efficiency and control.
