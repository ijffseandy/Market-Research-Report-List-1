<p><p>The Disposable Respiratory Masks market is primarily divided into two main types: Laryngeal Mask Airways (LMA) and face masks. LMA devices are used in medical settings to maintain airway patency during anesthesia or emergency situations, providing a secure airway without the need for endotracheal intubation. In contrast, face masks, including surgical masks and N95 respirators, are widely used in healthcare and general public settings for infection control and respiratory protection. Both types play a crucial role in preventing airborne diseases.</p></p>
