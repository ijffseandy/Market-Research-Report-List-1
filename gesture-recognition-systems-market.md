<p><strong>The Gesture Recognition Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygonal Imaging</li><li>Light Flying Time</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gesture recognition systems can be categorized into three main market types: Polygonal Imaging, Light Flying Time, and Structured Light. </p><p>Polygonal Imaging involves capturing hand shapes and movements through 2D image processing to interpret gestures. Light Flying Time utilizes time-of-flight sensors to measure distances and recognize gestures based on the time it takes for light to return. Structured Light projects patterns onto surfaces, enabling the system to analyze distortions and identify gestures accurately. Each technology offers unique advantages in various applications, including gaming, security, and human-computer interaction.</p></p>
