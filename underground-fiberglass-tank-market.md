<p><p>The underground fiberglass tank market is primarily categorized into two types: single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall underground fiberglass tanks are designed with a single layer, offering basic protection against leaks and spills. In contrast, double wall underground fiberglass tanks feature an additional outer layer that provides enhanced safety and environmental protection by containing any leaks from the inner tank, making them ideal for hazardous materials. Both types are widely used for storage in various industries due to their durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
