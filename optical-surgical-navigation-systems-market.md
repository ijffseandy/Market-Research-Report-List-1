<p><p>The Optical Surgical Navigation Systems market encompasses primarily two types: Hybrid Navigation and Optical Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines various technologies, such as optical and electromagnetic systems, to enhance surgical accuracy and provide real-time imaging, benefiting from the strengths of each method. In contrast, Optical Navigation relies primarily on advanced optical imaging techniques, utilizing cameras and projectors to track and visualize surgical instruments relative to anatomical structures. Both approaches aim to improve precision and outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
