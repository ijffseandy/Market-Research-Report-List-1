<p><p>The Surge Arrester Market is primarily divided into two types: polymeric and porcelain. Polymeric surge arresters typically feature a lightweight, durable design that offers excellent outdoor performance, flexibility, and insensitivity to moisture. They are increasingly favored for their ability to resist environmental stressors. In contrast, porcelain surge arresters are known for their robust structure and high dielectric strength, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in harsh conditions. Both types play a crucial role in protecting electrical systems from voltage surges.</p></p>
