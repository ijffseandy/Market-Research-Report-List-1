<p><ul><li>Permanent Packers</li><li>Retrievable Packers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packers are essential tools in the oil and gas industry, used to isolate sections of a wellbore. Permanent packers are designed for long-term installation, providing a reliable seal for the duration of the well's life. In contrast, retrievable packers can be removed and reused, offering flexibility for temporary applications or when workover operations are needed. The market for these devices is driven by exploration activity, technological advancements, and the need for efficient reservoir management in hydrocarbon extraction.</p></p>
