<p><p>The special lubricating oil market is primarily categorized into mineral-based lubricating oils and biobased lubricating oils. Mineral-based lubricating oils are derived from refining crude oil, offering excellent lubrication properties and high thermal stability, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, biobased lubricating oils are produced from renewable biological sources, promoting sustainability and reduced environmental impact. These oils are gaining traction due to increasing environmental awareness and regulations favoring eco-friendly products, catering to diverse sectors such as automotive and manufacturing.</p></p>
