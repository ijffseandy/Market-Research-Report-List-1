<p><p>Organo-modified siloxanes are available in three main market types: comb-like structure, linear structure, and combined structure. The comb-like structure features a silicon backbone with organic side chains, providing enhanced performance and compatibility. Linear structures consist of long, straight chains ideal for applications requiring flexibility and durability. Combined structures merge characteristics from both comb and linear types, offering versatile properties for specialized applications. Each structure serves distinct functions in industries like coatings, adhesives, and personal care products, catering to diverse performance needs.</p></p>
