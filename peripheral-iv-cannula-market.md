<p><p>The Peripheral IV Cannula market includes two primary types: Y-shaped and straight cannulas. Y-shaped cannulas feature a bifurcated design that allows for multiple infusion lines, enhancing medication delivery flexibility. This design is particularly useful in complex treatments where simultaneous infusions are required. Straight cannulas, on the other hand, are more traditional and typically used for straightforward intravenous access. They are favored for their simplicity and ease of use in standard infusion therapies, making them a staple in clinical settings.</p></p>
