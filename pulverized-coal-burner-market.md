<p><ul><li>Swirl Type Pulverized Coal Burner</li><li>Once-Through Pulverized Coal Burner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulverized coal burner market is primarily segmented into two types: swirl type pulverized coal burners and once-through pulverized coal burners. </p><p>Swirl type burners utilize a swirling airflow to enhance the mixing of coal particles with air, promoting efficient combustion and reducing emissions. In contrast, once-through burners operate with a single pass of coal and air, optimized for high combustion efficiency in specific applications. Both types are pivotal in industrial processes, particularly in power generation and cement production, ensuring effective energy use and minimized pollutants.</p></p>
