<p><ul><li>Below 20 μm</li><li>20-50 μm</li><li>Above 50 μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrodeposited copper foil market for printed circuit boards is categorized into three types based on thickness: below 20 μm, 20-50 μm, and above 50 μm. Foils below 20 μm are primarily used in high-density interconnect (HDI) applications, featuring advanced miniaturization. The 20-50 μm category is the most common, suitable for standard PCB applications. Foils above 50 μm are utilized in heavy-duty applications requiring higher conductivity and thermal performance, such as power electronics and automotive circuits.</p></p>
