<p><strong>The Mammalian Cell Banking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The mammalian cell banking market encompasses various types of cell banks crucial for biopharmaceuticals. Master Cell Banks (MCBs) are the primary repository of a cell line, ensuring consistency and quality. Working Cell Banks (WCBs) are derived from MCBs for production runs. End of Production Cell Banks (EOPCBs) archive cells after production completion, while Research & Development Cell Banks (R&D CBs) support experimental applications. Other cell banks may include specialized or customized collections for unique research needs, enhancing bioproduction and therapeutic development.</p></p>
