<p><p>The beverage stabilizers market includes various types that enhance texture and stability. Xanthan gum is a versatile thickener and emulsifier, widely used for its ability to maintain viscosity in both hot and cold liquids. Carboxymethyl cellulose acts as a thickening agent, improving mouthfeel and preventing separation. Carrageenan, derived from seaweed, is popular for its gelling properties, particularly in dairy and plant-based drinks. Gum Arabic, a natural resin, functions as a stabilizer and emulsifier, contributing to cloudiness and texture in beverages.</p></p>
