<p><p>Acrylic adhesives are categorized into several types based on their formulation and application. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and ease of cleanup, making them ideal for interior use. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding capabilities but often with higher volatility and odors. Reactive adhesives bond through chemical reactions, offering exceptional durability and heat resistance. Other market types might include specialty formulations such as UV-curable or pressure-sensitive adhesives, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
