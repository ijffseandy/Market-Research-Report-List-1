<p><p>Floating production systems are categorized based on water depth: shallow water, deepwater, and ultra-deepwater. Shallow water systems operate in depths up to 200 meters and are typically more cost-effective and easier to install. Deepwater systems function between 200 and 1,500 meters, requiring advanced technology and engineering due to harsher conditions. Ultra-deepwater systems exceed 1,500 meters, presenting significant technical challenges and demanding state-of-the-art solutions. Each market type addresses specific exploration and production needs, influencing investment and operational strategies.</p></p>
