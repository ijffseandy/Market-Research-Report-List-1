<p><p>Pet medical insurance is categorized into several types based on coverage duration. Lifetime cover provides annual coverage limits that renew each year, ensuring ongoing treatment for chronic conditions throughout a pet's life. Non-lifetime cover has a fixed limit that only applies to specific conditions for a certain time, leaving pets vulnerable once limits are reached. Other market options may include per-incident plans or accident-only coverage, tailoring to varying needs and budgets, allowing pet owners to select suitable protection for their pets.</p></p>
