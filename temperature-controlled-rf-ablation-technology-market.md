<p><p>Temperature-controlled RF ablation technology encompasses various methods for tissue ablation, leveraging precise temperature management. Ultrasound ablation uses high-frequency sound waves to generate thermal effects in targeted tissues. Laser ablation employs focused light energy for localized treatment. Electrical ablation applies radiofrequency energy to disrupt cellular function. Cryotherapy uses extreme cold to freeze and destroy abnormal tissues, while microwave ablation utilizes microwave energy to raise tissue temperature, leading to cell death. Each method offers unique advantages for specific clinical applications.</p></p>
