<p><p>The custom battery pack market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries are popular for their high energy density and longevity, making them ideal for portable electronics. Lithium Polymer (Li-Poly) batteries offer similar benefits with a flexible form factor. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries are known for their environmental friendliness and good capacity. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries provide reliable performance in extreme conditions but face disposal concerns. Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) batteries are robust and cost-effective for backup power solutions.</p></p>
