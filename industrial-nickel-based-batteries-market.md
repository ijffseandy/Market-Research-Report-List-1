<p><p>The industrial nickel-based batteries market primarily includes Nickel Cadmium (NiCd) and Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform in extreme temperatures, making them suitable for tools and backup power applications. Conversely, NiMH batteries offer higher energy density and are often used in hybrid vehicles and consumer electronics. Both types have distinct advantages, with NiCd being more resistant to overcharging while NiMH features lower environmental impact due to reduced toxic materials.</p></p>
