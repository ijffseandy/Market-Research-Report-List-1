<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) is a versatile compound utilized in various industrial applications, primarily in the form of black and green SiC. Black SiC, produced through a reduction process of silica and carbon, is mainly used in abrasives and refractory materials due to its high hardness. Green SiC, made through a similar silicon carbide production method but at higher temperatures, boasts higher purity and is preferred for advanced applications like ceramics, electronics, and semiconductors, given its exceptional thermal conductivity and strength.</p></p>
