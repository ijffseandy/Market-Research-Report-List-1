<p><strong>The Oil Well Perforating Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WCP Type</li><li>TCP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil well perforating gun market is primarily divided into two types: Well Completion Perforating (WCP) and Tubing Conveyed Perforating (TCP). WCP involves setting perforating guns in the wellbore during the completion phase to create channels through the casing into the reservoir. In contrast, TCP utilizes tubing as a means to convey perforating guns, allowing for deeper penetration and improved efficiency in perforation operations. Both types enhance oil flow and reservoir access while optimizing extraction processes.</p></p>
