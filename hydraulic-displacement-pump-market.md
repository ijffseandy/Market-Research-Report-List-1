<p><p>The hydraulic displacement pump market comprises primarily two types: axial piston pumps and radial piston pumps. Axial piston pumps utilize a series of pistons arranged parallel to the drive shaft, providing high efficiency and variable flow rates, ideal for mobile and industrial applications. Radial piston pumps, on the other hand, have pistons mounted radially around a central drive shaft, offering high pressure and robust performance in demanding environments. Both types play crucial roles in hydraulic systems across diverse industries.</p></p>
