<p><ul><li>Fixed Cycle Regulator</li><li>Variable Cycle Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power conditioner market is divided into two main types: fixed cycle regulators and variable cycle regulators. Fixed cycle regulators maintain a steady output voltage by using a consistent switching frequency, ensuring stable performance in applications with predictable power needs. In contrast, variable cycle regulators adjust their switching frequency based on the load's demand, optimizing efficiency and reducing energy loss in dynamic environments. Both types are essential for safeguarding sensitive equipment against voltage fluctuations and improving overall power quality.</p></p>
