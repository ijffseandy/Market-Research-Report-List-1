<p><ul><li>Gelling Agents</li><li>Friction Reducers</li><li>Surfactants</li><li>Corrosion & Scale Inhibitors</li><li>Acids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield stimulation chemicals market comprises various essential types. Gelling agents enhance the viscosity of fluids to improve hydrocarbon extraction. Friction reducers minimize resistance during fluid flow, increasing efficiency. Surfactants facilitate the interaction between liquids and solids, optimizing production. Corrosion and scale inhibitors protect equipment from damage and buildup, ensuring longevity. Acids are used for well stimulation, dissolving rock formations to enhance permeability. Together, these chemicals play a crucial role in improving oil and gas recovery processes.</p></p>
