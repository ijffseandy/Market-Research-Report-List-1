<p><p>The mask defect inspection equipment market comprises two main types: photomask inspection equipment and photomask substrate inspection equipment. Photomask inspection equipment focuses on identifying defects on the photomask itself, essential for ensuring high-quality production in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, photomask substrate inspection equipment examines the substrates used in photomasks to detect imperfections that could affect performance. Both types play a critical role in maintaining the integrity and reliability of photomasks, which are crucial for photolithography processes in advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
