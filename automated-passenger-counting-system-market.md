<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counting System (APCS) market includes various technologies to enhance public transport efficiency. Infrared systems use infrared beams to detect passenger movement. Stereoscopic vision relies on multiple cameras to create depth perception for accurate counts. Time-of-Flight technology measures distance by calculating the time light takes to travel, providing precise data. IP cameras leverage network connectivity to record and analyze passenger flow in real-time, allowing for advanced monitoring and data collection in transit systems.</p></p>
