<p><strong>The Door And Window Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Contact Sensors</li><li>Recessed Contact Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Door and window sensors are crucial in security systems, designed to detect unauthorized entries. Two primary types include surface contact sensors and recessed contact sensors. Surface contact sensors are mounted on door or window frames and can be easily installed, making them visible to potential intruders. In contrast, recessed contact sensors are embedded within the door or window frame, offering a discreet solution that blends into the surroundings. Both types enhance security by alerting users when entrances are breached.</p></p>
