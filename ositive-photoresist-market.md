<p><p>The positive photoresist market plays a crucial role in advanced lithography applications, particularly in processes like lift-off and array photo processes. In lift-off, a positive photoresist allows selective removal of materials by exposing and developing the resist, only leaving desired patterns. This technique is essential for fabricating intricate structures in electronics and microfabrication.
