<p><p>The marine bollards market comprises various types designed for securing boats and vessels. Tee bollards feature a T-shaped design, providing multiple mooring points. Horn bollards have two protruding horns for improved rope grip, while cleat bollards resemble traditional cleats, ideal for smaller vessels. Kidney bollards, with a kidney-shaped configuration, offer versatility in mooring, and pillar bollards are robust vertical structures suited for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different marine environments and vessel sizes.</p></p>
