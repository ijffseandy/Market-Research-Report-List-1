<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal vision camera market is segmented into three types based on wavelengths: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras. Short-wave infrared cameras (0.9-3 µm) are ideal for industrial applications, offering high resolution in specific conditions. Mid-wave infrared cameras (3-5 µm) are commonly used in surveillance and military operations, providing a balance of sensitivity and resolution. Long-wave infrared cameras (8-14 µm) excel in detecting heat signatures, making them suitable for diverse applications like firefighting and building inspections. Each type serves distinct markets and uses.</p></p>
