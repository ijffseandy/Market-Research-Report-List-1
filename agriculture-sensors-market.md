<p><p>The agriculture sensors market is categorized into three main types: physical sensors, mechanical sensors, and chemical sensors. Physical sensors monitor environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity, and soil moisture to optimize crop conditions. Mechanical sensors assess soil structure and compaction, providing data for improved planting and harvesting practices. Chemical sensors detect soil nutrients and contaminants, helping farmers manage fertilizer applications and ensure soil health. Together, these sensors enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability through precise monitoring and informed decision-making.</p></p>
