<p><ul><li>Milling</li><li>Turning</li><li>Electrical Discharge Machining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision machining market encompasses various techniques, including milling, turning, and electrical discharge machining (EDM). Milling is a process that removes material using rotating cutting tools, ideal for creating complex shapes and contours. Turning involves rotating a workpiece against a stationary cutting tool, commonly used for producing cylindrical parts. EDM uses electrical discharges to erode material, allowing for intricate designs in hard metals. Each method serves specific manufacturing needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in producing high-quality components.</p></p>
